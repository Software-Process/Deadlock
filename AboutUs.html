About This Website

What is this website?

This is an original and friendly question-and-answer website. To be part of the community, you just need to create an account. However, there is nothing to worry about: everything on this website is completely free and always will be. To create an account, click here, it is simple to do and very fast! Only an email address, a password and a username is required.

What question can I ask?

This website is specialized anything in particular. Anyone could ask about anything. From what color matches with your jacket to what is the meaning of life, the possibilities are endless!

Who answers the questions?

Anyone is welcomed to answer the questions and share their knowledge. Just remember that there is no room for pointless flame-wars. So keep it clean, will you?

What are those numbers and arrows on the right of the questions and answers?

This is a voting system. Sometimes, people post very good and interesting questions or solutions and they should get rewarded. This is where the voting system is taken into consideration. As an user, you have the power to upvote the most helpful questions/answers while to downvote those that are biased or unnecessary. The higher the grading is, the more likely that the question would reach the "Hot topics" page.
